## [v14](./../pdf/UBIQUITY%20v14_v1.pdf)

<details>
<summary>Key Features & Enhancements (v14) â€” click to expand</summary>

- **Sub-device Access:** Manual definition and direct access to subnetwork devices (PLCs, drives, IPCs) via VPN and embedded browser.
